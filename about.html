Our Mission to End Hunger and Reduce Waste

Connecting compassion with need, one meal at a time

Our Story

Nourish Nation was founded in 2020 when our founder, Riya Sharma, witnessed the heartbreaking paradox of food being wasted while people went hungry just blocks away.

What began as a small initiative to connect local restaurants with shelters has grown into a nationwide movement with over 500 partners and thousands of volunteers.

We believe that no edible food should go to waste while people are hungry, and that everyone deserves access to nutritious meals with dignity.

Our Core Values

Compassion

We treat all people with dignity and respect, recognizing that everyone deserves access to good food.

Sustainability

We're committed to reducing food waste's environmental impact through responsible redistribution.

Community

We believe in building bridges between businesses, organizations, and individuals.
